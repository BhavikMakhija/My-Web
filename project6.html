Pictionary

After discovering that my mom was having trouble finding a way to play pictionary online for a digital party, I came up with a digital version of the popular game. By using elements found within js and the p5 library, I was able to give the user 60 seconds to make a drawing. This game was a hit and was played by over 150 people at my mom's company.
